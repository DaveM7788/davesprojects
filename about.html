About


Howdy! I'm Dave. I am a mechanical engineer by degree and have experience in software engineering. My goal is to combine these talents to deliver powerful and intuitive solutions for problems I've encountered in everyday life.

This website, davesprojects.net, is a collection of these solutions in the form of software and hardware projects. Hopefully, you find that many of these projects are useful and easy to use. Most of the projects are focused on physical fitness and productivity, but I have a wide range of interests. I use each of these projects frequently and am committed to updating them. I highly appreciate feedback and will likely incorporate suggestions into future updates.

All of the projects are free. So go ahead and try a few out. I hope you enjoy them!
