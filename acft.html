Passing the ACFT requires a score of at least 60 in each event. Scores range from 0 to 600. The Army recently updated to ACFT 3.0. This update allows a timed plank to substitute for leg tucks. It also gets rid of the black, gray, and gold standards. There are now tiers based on how well you do relative to everyone else. Platinum is top 1%, Gold = top 10%, Silver = top 25%, Bronze = top 50%, Green = bottom 50%. The minimum passing score is not effected by gender however the tiers are.

For more information and training advice, see https://www.army.mil/acft/
