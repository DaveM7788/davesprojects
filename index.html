Fittest Fire

Fittest Fire a mobile app and game where you workout to level up. You earn points by logging workouts in the app. These points are then used in the game to level up.

Fittest Fire is a larger project and has its own website which can be visited by clicking here or the image below.

To-Do Alarm

Never forget your to-do list again! To-Do alarm is an Android app that combines an alarm clock with a to-do list. Everytime the alarm goes off you are greeted by name and your to-do list is automatically read to you.

The latest update now integrates your Google calendar. Each morning your calendar events are added to your to-do list. This update also adds a timer, stopwatch, and displays a randomly selected motivational quote every morning.

Cheetah HIIT

Amp up your high intensity interval training workouts! Cheetah HIIT is an Android app for timing and recording high intensity interval training. You can create custom time intervals and save workouts by type.

Cheetah HIIT can also run indefinitely if you don't know how many intervals you want to do. High intensity interval training is a great way to burn calories and mix up your routine. So download Cheetah HIIT today!

Army ACFT Calc

The U.S. Army updated its fitness test to the Army Combat Fitness Test (ACFT). Calculate your score on the web and on Android.

Ctrl-P MP3

Listen to your music with fuzzy song search on any LAMP based server. Ctrl-P MP3 supports uploading music, creating playlists, and all your standard audio controls.

Ctrl-P MP3 is free and open source. Contributors are always welcome.
